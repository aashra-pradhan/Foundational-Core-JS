One thing that you NEED to understand is, in coding you'll never reach that point that 
you know everything. Bassically garne k ho is, afu le project banaune kram ma kei kura/
feature/concept chahiyo bhane research garne, try out garne, error aaye ni chatgpt chadai cha,
copy paste garera k error bhayo sodhne.

adkine nai haina katai pani. kasailai ni sabai kura aaudaina, projects gardai gare pachi afai sakincha.
Practise hudai gaye pachi afai haat chalcha. Etti proficient bhaye pugcha ki timlai
coding resources use garera project banauna saknuparyo, sabai yaad nai hunu parne hoina, sabai concepts
aaunaii parne hoina!

For eg., for loop haruko variations, also different methods hunchan of array/string/class/object etc etc, class/object kai different
features hola, inheritance, 'extends' keyword, getter setter hola, tini haru timle chatgpt garyau bhane concept majale bujhchau,
so project banauda chahiye bela ma research gara. sabai ratta marnuparne kadapi haina.
Use gardai gayepachi concept yaad hunu arkai kura ho, huncha pani.
